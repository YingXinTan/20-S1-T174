FAQ

What features can I use on this app?

The app allows you to book flights.
Features include a reminder for upcoming flights, an interactive map that allows you to visualise flight routes, allows you to choose
waypoints for your flight. The app also includes a schedule log that displays past, future and nearest flight bookings.
You can also delete future flights.


How do I book a flight?

In the booking page (main page), you can input your travel date, time and origin airport.
After clicking the confirm button, you will be brought to the fleet information page that contains an interactive map and information on all available flights.
In this page, you will select available airplanes, waypoints and destination on respective markers using the interactive map.
After selection, click the "CONFIRM" button down below.
Your selected flight will be booked.


How does the interactive map work?

The map allows users to visualise flight route when booking a flight.
Steps to book flight on map.
1. Click on the origin marker (grey) to start booking process.
2. Airport markers with available airplanes will show, click to select.
3. Waypoint markers within selected airplane's range will show, click to select.
4. If no waypoint is needed or desired destination is in range, user has to click "SET DESTINATION" button.
5. Destination markers will appear on map. Click to select.


Where can I view all my flight bookings?

Click on the navigation drawer on the top left of the page, look for the "Schedule log" and click on it.
This page will display all booked flights.
You are able to view and delete flights here.


How can I view my next scheduled flights?

A reminder section can be found at the bottom of the main page.
Details of the next upcoming flight are to be shown in this section.


Where can I view information about the planes available?

Click on the navigation drawer on the top left of the page, look for the "Fleet Information" and click on it.
All the plane summary can be found here. User can view the summary according to the preferred sorting.
